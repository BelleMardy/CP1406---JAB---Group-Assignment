About Us

The Barrier Reef Orchestra is a product of North Queensland Ensembles Inc which is an Incorporated Association registered in the State of Queensland. It is also a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. It is based in Townsville, North Queensland, Australia.

History of the Orchestra

Over a two-year period in the late nineties, Townsville musicians, arts workers and a group of people who had a love for music realised the potential for developing a community-based Orchestra, one that would flourish and become an integral part of the City’s cultural life. In 2000 to ‘test’ the viability of establishing an Orchestra a weekend workshop was arranged and musicians were invited to participate. This resulted in the establishment of the Barrier Reef Orchestra. Now in a typical year the Barrier Reef Orchestra performs three times generally with a guest conductor and occasionally with other performing groups. In addition, smaller ensembles, using players from the Orchestra, regularly give concerts. There is also a concert organised for the Australian Festival Of Chamber Music and the Orchestra contributes to the Australian Concert Vocal Competition.
